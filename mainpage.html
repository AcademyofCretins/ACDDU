Academy of Cretins

Our Mission

"At the Academy of Cretins, our Mission is to introduce classical piano to the world."

We do not expect any of our students to become professionals, nor do we expect our students to understand classical piano music completely. We want to introduce classical piano music to them so students can decide if it really is the right thing for them; if they can pursue it as a hobby.
lWe want to make it clear that we only serve as a temporary bridge to lead the world into classical piano music.

The Importance of experiencing Classical Music

Classical music has a lot of unexpected benefits.

  • Classical music increases physical performance.
  • Classical music eases chronic pain.
  • Classical music lowers stress.
  • Classical music enhances your thinking.
  • Classical music enhances memory.
  • Classical music decreases blood pressure.
  • Classical music enhances creativity.

Benefits of playing the piano

  • Playing piano improves the flexibility of the hand.
  • Piano can be learnt quickly (compared to other instruments) and it is relatively easy to improve at piano playing.
  • Piano playing is unlikely to cause injuries.
  • The piano is a very popular instrument that can make a wide variety of sounds.
